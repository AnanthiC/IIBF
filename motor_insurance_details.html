Motor Private Car Insurance Policy

SBI General Insurance offers a comprehensive Motor Private Car Insurance Policy to protect your vehicle against loss, damage, theft, and third-party liabilities. This policy ensures that you are covered for unforeseen incidents, providing peace of mind on the road.

Key Features:

  • Coverage against accidental damage and theft
  • Third-party liability coverage
  • Personal accident cover for the driver
  • Optional add-ons like roadside assistance, engine protection, etc.
